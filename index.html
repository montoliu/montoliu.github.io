Welcome to my web page

I am a member of the Departamento de Ingeniería y Ciencia de los Computadores of the Jaume I University (UJI) [Map], Castellón, Spain.

I belong to the iNIT research institute. My actual main research interest are: Indoor localization and navigation, Game AI and Machine learning

Past research interest include: Sport analysis, Smart cities, Human and social behavior from sensor data, Image registration, Motion estimation and segmentation, Human detection and Human action recognition.
