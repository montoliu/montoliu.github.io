Online Courses


Introducción a la informática: Codificación de la información

The objective of this course is to explain how today's computers encode information. Among other issues, it will cover how integers (positive and negative) real numbers and alphanumeric characters are encoded. For this, different numbering systems such as decimal, binary and hexadecimal systems are explained.

The course is in Spanish.

First and second versions of this online course was held in the MOOC UJI platform.

Materials of the course are accesible in:

  • UJI OCW platform.
  • Videos are also accesible here.
  • There is a book covering all the material.
