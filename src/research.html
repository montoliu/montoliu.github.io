Research

I belong to the GIANT research group from the iNIT research institute. My actual main research interest are: Machine learning, e-Health, Game AI and Indoor localization.

Past research interest include: Sport analysis, Smart cities, Human and social behavior from sensor data, Image registration, Motion estimation and segmentation, Human detection and Human action recognition.
